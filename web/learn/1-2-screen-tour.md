# A 2-minute tour of the screen

Before we *do* anything, let's just name the parts of the screen so nothing feels mysterious. You don't have to click any of these yet — just find them with your eyes.

## The left sidebar

Everything you navigate with lives down the left.

**At the top** is a small strip of icons:

- **Datacenter** (the house icon) — your home screen. The big overview of everything.
- **App Store** — a catalogue of ready-to-install apps.
- **Issues** — a list of things WolfStack thinks you should look at.
- **Predictive Inbox** — early warnings *before* something becomes a problem.
- **Apps & Tools** (the grid icon) — a drawer holding all the more advanced screens, tucked away so they don't clutter things.

**Below that** is your **Servers** list. Every machine WolfStack manages shows up here. Click one to see what's on it.

**At the very bottom** are small round buttons:

- **Settings** (the cog)
- **Help & Docs** (the question mark)
- **Getting Started** (the compass) — that's *this course*. It lives here so you can always come back.
- **Add Server** (the plus)
- **Sign Out** (the door)

## The main area

Everything to the right of the sidebar is the **main area**. Whatever you click in the sidebar opens here. The title at the top-left of the main area always tells you where you are.

## What to ignore (for now)

Here's the permission you came for: **ignore the App Store, Issues, Predictive Inbox, and the whole Apps & Tools drawer for now.**

For the next few lessons, the only two parts of the screen that matter are:

1. your **Servers** list, and
2. the **Getting Started** compass (so you can find your way back here).

That's the whole screen. Nothing here can bite you.

## ✓ What you just learned

- The **sidebar** (left) is how you get around; the **main area** (right) is what you're looking at.
- The top icons and the Apps & Tools drawer are for later — you can ignore them.
- This course lives on the **compass** in the bottom-left, always one click away.
