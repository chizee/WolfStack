# The Issues page (your morning check)

You don't need to babysit your servers. You need one place that tells you "here's what's worth your attention" — and ideally fixes some of it for you. That's the **Issues** page. Make checking it your 30-second morning habit.

## Open it

Click **Issues** in the top of the sidebar (the warning-triangle icon).

You'll see a **card for each of your servers**. At a glance, each card tells you how healthy that server is using coloured chips:

- a **red** chip — *critical*, things to look at first
- a **yellow** chip — *warnings*, worth a look
- a **blue** chip — *info*, minor notes
- a green **✓ All clear** — nothing needs you

If a server is **behind on its WolfStack version**, the card also shows an **Upgrade** button so you can bring it up to date right there.

## Fix things in one click

This is the part people love. Many issues come with a fix button right next to them — labelled **Clean** or **Repair**:

- **Clean** — tidy-up jobs, like clearing space that logs have eaten.
- **Repair** — corrective fixes, like restarting a service that should be running.

Click it, and WolfStack does the fix and re-checks. You'll see the button spin, then a confirmation, then the card refreshes with the problem gone. No terminal, no guesswork.

> Not every issue has a one-click fix — some need a human decision, and WolfStack won't pretend otherwise. But a surprising number of the routine annoyances can be cleared right from this page.

## How to use it

Make it a rhythm, not a chore:

1. Open **Issues** once a day (or whenever something feels off).
2. Deal with **red** first, then **yellow**, ignore **blue** unless you're curious.
3. Click **Clean**/**Repair** where offered; note anything that needs a real decision for later.

That's genuinely most of "keeping servers healthy" for a small setup.

## ✓ What you just learned

- **Issues** (top sidebar) shows a health card per server with **red / yellow / blue** chips or **✓ All clear**.
- Many issues have a one-click **Clean** or **Repair** button that fixes and re-checks.
- An **Upgrade** button appears when a server is behind.
- Treat it as a quick daily glance: handle red, then yellow, move on.
